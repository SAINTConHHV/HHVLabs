Walkthrough

gqrx basics

Make sure the rtl-sdr is plugged in to a USB port, then run gqrx -e (the -e means you want to edit settings).

This will pop up a window to select which kind of SDR you are using

  1. In the Device field, choose the Realtek SD option for the devices we have at the conference
  2. Set Input Rate to an appropriate vaule (higher will cover more of the band)
  3. Click OK

Now, tune the receiver to somewhere you will be able to see the frequency you are interested in and press the button in the top left that looks like a play button (or a power button, depending on the version of gqrx). To tune, scroll the mouse wheel with the mouse cursor over the digit you want to change on the frequency display. The red line is where we are tuned to (for purposes of demodulation), the light dotted line in the center of the waveform display is the center frequency that the hackrf is tuned to. On the right pane in the 'Receiver Options' pane, you will see an offset from the center frequency.

find an FM radio broadcast

  1. make sure your antenna is an appropriate length
  2. choose WFM(mono) for demodulation on the right
  3. Tune to somewhere between 88 and 108 MHz
