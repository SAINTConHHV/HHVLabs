ADS-B with adsbox

Goals

In this lab, you will see one of the applications for software defined radio. You will use ADSBox software along with an RTL-SDR dongle to track airplanes currently flying overhead by decoding their ADS-B beacons. The software will tune the radio, decode the beacon and plot plane location on a map.

Background Information

Software defined radio takes the processing of radio signals out of hardware and moves it to software. This makes it possible to decode and process many different radio protocols with a single receiver. RTL-SDR is a SDR solution that leverages a cheap (~$30) digital TV-tuner and can tune into signals from 22MHz - 2.2GHz (varies depending on the specific dongle you purchase).
